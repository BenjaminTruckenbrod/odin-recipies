BLT

Description

The BLT is a classic sandwich that is mostly eaten at lunch. It relies on simple ingredients to highlight big flavor. This recipe will explain to you how to make a perfect BLT. Its easy so there is no chance you will mess it up. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 tbls mayonaise
  • 1/2 beefsteak tomato
  • 2 leaves romaine lettuce
  • 2-3 strips bacon

Steps

  1. Cook bacon
  2. Spread mayonaise on both slices of bread
  3. Rip lettuce into pieces sized for your sandwich and add them to your sandwich
  4. Slice tomato into flat slices and add to your sandwich
  5. Cut bacon strips in half and add them to your sandwich
  6. Close your sandwitch with the top piece of bread
  7. Cut sandwich in half and enjoy
