Not Your Mother's Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a breakfast dish that never fails. This recipie will show you a way to shake things up a little bit a add new flavors to your oatmeal. I was inspired by rice pudding to add turmeric, and from there test rest of the ingredients followed

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp
  • 2 dates
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/4 cup walnuts
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 dashes salt

Steps

  1. Add salt, spices and water to a small pot and bring to a boil
  2. While water is boiling, chop dates and bananas
  3. When the water comes to a boil add oats and reduce heat to a simmer
  4. Add fruit
  5. When most of the water remove from heat and add to a bowl
  6. Garnish with crushed walnuts
  7. Enjoy this delicious recipe and experiment with add-ins
